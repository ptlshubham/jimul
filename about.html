ABOUT US

Welcome to Jimul, where we offer a unique and delicious selection of flavoured and roasted dry fruits. Our premium quality products are perfect for those who want to indulge in healthy snacking without compromising on taste.

At Jimul, we take pride in our range of exotic flavours, including our popular Peri Peri Sing, Salt & Pepper Kaju, Dryfruit Chevda, Peri Peri Kaju, Rajwadi Masala Sing, Salted Roasted Badam, Masala Kaju, and Rajwadi Kaju. Each flavour is carefully crafted to provide a unique taste experience that will tantalize your taste buds and leave you wanting more.

We believe that healthy snacking should be fun and exciting, which is why we offer a variety of products to suit all tastes and preferences. Our roasted dry fruits are packed with essential nutrients, including protein, fiber, and healthy fats, making them the perfect snack to fuel your day.

Mission

In addition to enhancing human life, our food processing company is committed to being honest, having strong moral principles, and providing superior healthy food to all privileged consumers.

Vision

We can control and monitor the quality and origin of nuts by adding a constant value to the dry fruit industry and by managing the challenges posed by the current market environment. The rest of our product line is in this segment. We produce high-quality garments with no competition in taste in the Indian market.

History

We started our company in 2013 on a smaller scale and low capacity of cashews. We started from a 10 sqft shop and later expanded to 100 sqft with the help of our customers and staff. We gradually grew year after year with a huge place and an arsenal of products under our brand name "Jimul".
